BASKETBALL CAMP OVERVIEW

IMG Academy boys basketball camps incorporate small-group training sessions as well as position-specific drills that prepare players of all levels for their upcoming tryouts, league season, and even for the collegiate level. The same coaching staff who led IMG to the 2019 GEICO National Championship game works directly with IMG campers to hone their technical, tactical, and strategic skills during each session.

Camp programs include on-court group sessions that focus on shooting, dribbling, defense, and more. All basketball campers will receive a post-camp analysis report inclusive of coach feedback and important takeaways.
